Tower servers

Tower servers are a type of server form factor that comes as a standalone, upright cabinet, also known as a chassis configuration. Tower servers are best deployed by smaller organizations that don’t need a full data center. Ideal for someone looking to use server in a small company. Does not require high maintenance.

Rack Servers

A rack server, also known as a rack mount server, blade servers or rack mount computer design for data centers and NOC operations.

Blade servers

Blade servers are most commonly used by larger data centers because they have a strong need to maximize space and power capacity utilization and efficiency, have high computing requirements, and can support a higher thermal and electrical load.
